Top to bottom

Closest-to-user at the top, deepest substrate at the bottom. The workbench is where humans and agents meet; the product master is what they reason against.

The middle layer

Bluestone is deliberately runtime-agnostic. Agents run wherever the customer wants — Claude, GPT, Gemini, internal — and operate against Bluestone through MCP and task-level APIs.

The right tags

Every layer says any X. Openness is structural, not a feature. This is the line that separates Bluestone from PIMs that bolted a single proprietary AI assistant onto the UI.

The underpinning

The Standard is the cross-cutting trust commitment — the 11-point standard for agent-ready PIM, the L5 parity bar, MACH certification. It anchors everything above.
