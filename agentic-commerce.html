What is an MCP Server?

Beyond a one-way feed, MCP allows agents to "ask" your PIM specific questions. It surfaces the exact logic and data required to resolve complex customer queries the moment they arise.

How it works

Rather than agents "scraping" your website or waiting for a feed update, MCP provides a secure, interactive interface. It allows AI to query your product logic and technical specs directly—pulling only the relevant facts to answer a specific user intent.

The Result?

•

On-Demand Precision: Provides structured specs (rather than marketing fluff) for confident AI comparisons.

•

Informed Reasoning: Includes direct links so users can verify details and purchase products instantly.
