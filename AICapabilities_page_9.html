The skills

A skill for every part of the job.

Follow a product through the platform and the skills line up with the work — complete it, translate it, picture it, describe it, check it, route it. Each one is its own tool; the set keeps growing.

AI Enrich

Turns raw supplier data into market-ready records — fills attributes, drafts descriptions, closes gaps, across markets.

AI Linguist

Translates and localises product content for every market. Brand voice preserved, glossaries respected.

AI DAM

Generative product imagery, and more — it can read product images and generate attributes and content from what's in them.

AI Templates

Predefined templates for recurring work. Set a task up once — its prompt, rules and format — and your team just picks the template and runs it. No re-writing instructions for every product.

AI Analyst

Spots data quality issues and recommends fixes — checking your catalogue against the standards you set.

AI Workflow

Automates approval routing and exception handling across your product workflows, with people on the loop.

And how you trust it

Powerful AI raises one question: can I trust the data?

The more your AI generates, the more that question matters. AI Analyst is the answer. It continuously checks your catalogue against the standards you set — surfacing quality issues, missing values, and miscategorised products, whether the data came from a supplier, your team, or another AI skill. It doesn't just flag problems; it recommends the fix.

So the rest of the set can do the heavy lifting, and you keep the confidence that what reaches your channels is complete, consistent, and correct.
