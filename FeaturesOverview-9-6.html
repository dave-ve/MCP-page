AI, built in — not bolted on

Automate the work. Your team makes the calls.

AI can take on onboarding, enrichment and validation, so they don't wait on someone to start them. It handles the volume; your people handle the judgement — and every change is logged across people and agents alike.

Onboard

Agents read, map and enrich incoming supplier feeds, so new ranges aren't gated by hours your team can spare.

Enrich & translate

Descriptions, attributes and localisation generated to your standards, ready for every market at once.

Validate

Errors caught the moment they arrive, not in a periodic review — quality holds as volume climbs.
