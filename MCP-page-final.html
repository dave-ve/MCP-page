The Model Context Protocol

How MCP Connects AI
to Your Products

The Model Context Protocol is an open standard that lets AI models connect securely and communicate with external data. Developed to eliminate the "context gap," MCP allows AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini to interact with your product data as if it were part of their native knowledge.

👁️
Context — the eyes to see

AI sees your catalogue live

MCP gives AI models a live, semantic window into your catalogue. No stale exports — agents see your product truth in real time, exactly as it exists in your PIM.

🤲
Tools — the hands to act

AI acts on your data directly

Beyond reading data, MCP tools let the internal AI Agent trigger executable functions — updating attributes, enriching records, running bulk jobs — directly inside the PIM workflow.

🔗
Sampling — the voice to reason

One Open Standard, Every AI

MCP is an open standard backed by Anthropic. Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity — and whatever comes next — all connect through the same protocol. No custom integrations, no rebuilding as the AI landscape shifts.
