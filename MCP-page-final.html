What is MCP?

The Model Context Protocol is an open standard that lets AI models connect securely to external data. Developed to eliminate the "context gap," MCP allows AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini to interact with your product data as if it were part of their native knowledge. Bluestone PIM ships a native MCP server, so your data is available to AI out of the box.

👁️
